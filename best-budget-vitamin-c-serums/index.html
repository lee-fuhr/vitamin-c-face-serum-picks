Vitamin C serums are a skincare staple: they brighten skin tone, fade dark spots, boost collagen, and defend against environmental damage. But finding one that actually works without blowing your budget can be tricky. A good budget serum should contain a stable form of vitamin C (often L-ascorbic acid or a derivative), a concentration that is effective without causing irritation, and supporting ingredients like vitamin E or hyaluronic acid. When shopping on a budget, look for a formula that lists vitamin C early in the ingredients and comes in opaque, airless packaging to keep it fresh. Cheaper serums sometimes skimp on stability or use synthetic fragrances, so reading user feedback about texture and smell can help you dodge duds. The three serums below are consistently praised for delivering visible results without the prestige price tag.
